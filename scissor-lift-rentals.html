Typical applications for scissor lifts

Warehouse & logistics Racking installation, inventory systems, lighting, fire-fighting pipelines and general maintenance at height.
Factory & industrial Cable trays, ducting, air lines, production utilities and maintenance above work areas.
Commercial buildings Atrium access, ceiling work, mall interiors, signage, hotel and office maintenance.
Fit-out & interior contracts HVAC, electrical, false ceiling and interior finishing works where access must be neat and controlled.

Why choose a scissor lift over ladders or scaffolding?

A scissor lift provides a stable, guarded work platform that can be moved quickly between locations, saving setup time and reducing manual handling.

Stable guarded platform Guardrails and solid platforms improve safety compared to basic ladders or makeshift arrangements.
Faster setup and relocation Move between work points by simply lowering, driving and elevating again – no repeated scaffolding assembly.
Multiple people with tools Platform capacities allow technicians to work together with tools and materials at height.

We recommend scissor lifts wherever there is repetitive work at the same height with a clear vertical path and suitable ground conditions.
