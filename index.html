How our rental process works

A simple, clear process to help you lock in the right equipment with transparent pricing and support throughout the rental period.

  • Step 1 – Share your requirement Tell us the working height, application, surface type, and access conditions. Share site photos or drawings if available.
  • Step 2 – Get equipment suggestion & quote We propose suitable machines (boom, scissor, man lift) and send a commercial offer based on your duration.
  • Step 3 – Confirm rental & scheduling Finalise dates, confirm whether operator is required, and plan mobilisation and demobilisation.
  • Step 4 – On-site support during rental Support for minor technical issues, operator coordination, and rental extensions if your project requires more time.
